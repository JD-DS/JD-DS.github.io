Bio

Before my journey into data science, I served as a paratrooper in the United States Army Airborne. This experience instilled in me a strong sense of discipline, teamwork, and resilience; as well as allowing me to bring unique perspective to problem-solving from my experiences in navigating high-pressure situations while maintaining a strategic, results-oriented approach.

Post military service, I studied Business and Finance at Fordham University, where I developed a keen understanding of economic trends and corporate strategy. I then earned a Master's in Data Science from the University of Virginia which included coursework in Statistics, Computer Science, Machine Learning, Big Data Systems, and working as a student researcher for a project in the discovery of material physics using computer vision and deep learning. I also interned in Quantitative Analytics at a major bank.
